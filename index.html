What We Do

Full-Spectrum
Production Design

3D Stage Design

Full conceptual and production-ready 3D stage designs. From initial sketch to final render, we create stages that command attention and serve your production flawlessly.

Lighting Design

Pre-visualized lighting systems including moving heads, lasers, LED walls, and atmospheric effects. See your show before a single fixture is hung.

LED & Visual Systems

Custom LED wall configurations, pixel mapping, and media server integration. Immersive visual environments that transform the audience experience completely.

Event Production

End-to-end production design for festivals, corporate events, tours, and private shows. Technical riders, ground plans, and full production documentation.

Structural Engineering

Certified structural calculations for stage systems, rigging points, and truss configurations. Safety and spectacle in perfect balance every time.

3D Visualization

Photorealistic renders and walkthroughs for client presentations. Help stakeholders understand exactly what their event will look and feel like.
